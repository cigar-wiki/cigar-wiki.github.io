### Brand Notes

**Status**

Juan López is a current pre-revolution brand, established in the 1870s. It is classified by Habanos SA in their "Other" marketing category. In the pre-2019 categorisation system, it was deemed a Local brand. It has a minor market share, although is a popular choice for regional editions.

**Generally**

The range comprises medium to full strength cigars using tobacco from the premium _Vuelta Abajo_ region. There is a non-Cuban brand with the same name.
