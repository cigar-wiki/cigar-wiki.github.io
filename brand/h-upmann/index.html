H. Upmann

Brand Notes​

Status
H. Upmann is a current pre-Revolution brand, established in 1844. It is now classified by Habanos SA as a Global brand, but prior to c2007 it was only classified as a Multi-local. It has a significant market share.

Generally
The range comprises light to medium strength cigars, using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region. There is a non-Cuban brand with the same name.

Current Production
Until 2008, all the current production cigars were pre-1960s releases. Since then there have been subsequent releases.

Special Releases
Special pre-1960 packaging was discontinued in the 1980s. Special Releases commenced in 1999 with the inclusion of H. Upmann cigars in the Siglo XXI Humidor. Since 2004 there have been further Special Releases.


Standard Band A
Pre-1960s to c2006 -- Discontinued. Not embossed.


Standard Band B
Pre-1960s to c2006 -- Discontinued. Embossed.


Standard Band C
c2006 on -- Current. Embossed.
