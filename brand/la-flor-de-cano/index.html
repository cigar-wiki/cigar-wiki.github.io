Brand Notes​

Status

La Flor de Cano is a current pre-revolution brand, established in 1884.

Generally

It is classified by Habanos SA to be in their 'Other Brands' marketing category. Under the pre-2019 system, it was classified as a Local brand. It has only a few cigars in production and a minor market share. It does, however, receive the occasional Regional Edition release.

The range comprises medium strength cigars, using tobacco blended from the Vuelta Abajo and Semi Vuelta regions.

There is a non-Cuban brand with a similar name.

Standard Band A

1960s on -- Current

Embossed.
