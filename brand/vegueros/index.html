Brand Notes​

Status

Vegueros is a post-revolution Habanos SA brand.

It was established in 1997. All vitolas in the range were discontinued in 2012, and the brand was relaunched with all new cigars with updated branding in 2013.

It is classified as a Volume Portfolio brand at a lower price than other brands.

Generally

The range comprises tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.

The cigars are a full strength blend.

Vegueron - a new special release size

Construction:

Handmade.

Bands:

Various.

Packaging:

Numbered humidor of 135 cigars (135 produced).

Status:

A 2003 release. 2003-01-01 A private commission by Caracol SA of Cuba.

History:

Commemorates the 135th Anniversary of the establishment of the City of Pinar del Rio, known as the "Capital of Cuban Tobacco". Signed by five leading cultivators.

The "Vegueron" cigar in this humidor is called the "Cepo" by many sources. Reportedly, this humidor was originally intended to hold Belicosos Extra cigars, however, this was changed at the last minute at the request of Caracol SA, who commissioned the humidors. Prominentes sized cigars were rolled, and then cut down to fit the space in the humidor, creating a unique factory size. The cigar has no official name, however was referred to as "Vegueron" ("Big Vegueros") internally by the factory. "Cepo" would appear to be a misreading of text referring to the ring gauge.

Brown 135th Aniversario Humidor release of Cuban cigars.
