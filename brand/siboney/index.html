Brand Notes​

Status

Siboney is a Discontinued brand.

It was a pre-revolution brand probably initially producing only cigarettes, with cigars introduced in the 1940/1950s. It was discontinued shortly after the Cuban revolution.

The brand was reinstated in 1988 to produce mini-cigarillos for the Austrian market in partnership with Austria Tabak. One model of 'premium' cigar was also produced. The line was not particularly successful, and was discontinued in 1996.
