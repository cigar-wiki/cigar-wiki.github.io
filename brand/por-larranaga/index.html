Brand Notes​

Status

Por Larrañaga is a current pre-revolution brand, established by Ignacio Larrañaga in 1834.

It is classified by Habanos SA in their "Other" category of Portfolio brands. Under the pre-2019 classification system it was considered a Local brand.

Generally

The range comprises light to medium strength cigars, using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.
