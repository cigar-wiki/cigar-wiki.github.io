Brand Notes​

Status

El Rey del Mundo is a current pre-revolution brand, established in 1882.

Generally

It is classified by Habanos SA in their Other category of portfolio brands.

The range comprises light to medium strength cigars using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.

There is a non-Cuban brand with the same name.
