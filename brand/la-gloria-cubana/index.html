Brand Notes​

Status

La Gloria Cubana is a current pre-revolution brand that was established in 1885. It lapsed in 1959 and was reinstated circa 1965.

It is classified by Habanos SA in their 'Other' marketing category. Prior to 2019 it was considered a local brand.

Generally

The range comprises medium strength cigars using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.

There is a non-Cuban brand with the same name.
