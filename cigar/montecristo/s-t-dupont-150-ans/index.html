Montecristo L'Esprit S. T. Dupont 150 Ans

Commemorative Release

Preciosos - a new special release size.

Cigar Name:

L'Esprit

Factory Name:

Preciosos

Dimensions:

Ring gaugerg50 × LengthMMT145 mm(5¾″)

Estimated Weight:

WeightGRM13.81g

Construction:

Handmade.

Bands:

Special band D with a Gold L'Esprit band, with an S.T. Dupont 150 Ans footband.

Packaging:

Numbered humidor of 50 cigars (600 produced).

Status:

A 2023 release. 2023-01-01Le Crépuscule humidor released mid-2024.

History:

Celebrates the 150th anniversary of the founding of French lighter and accessories manufacturer S. T. Dupont. A total of 600 humidors released, 200 each of three different designs named Le Crépuscule, La Nuit and L’Aurore. S.T. Dupont also released matching accessories in the same designs as each humidor (ashtray, lighters, cutter, cigar stand).

The factory size Preciosos is the same dimensions as the Maduro No.3, but is considered a separate vitola apparently due to differences in construction.
