Cohiba Lanceros VIP Gifts

Special Events

Cigar Name:

Lanceros

Factory Name:

Laguito No.1

Dimensions:

Ring gaugerg38 × LengthMMT192 mm(7½″)

Official Weight:

WeightGRM10.29g

Construction:

Handmade.

Bands:

Standard bands B, C, D, E, and sometimes 'standard' custom bands.

Packaging:

Dress box of 25 cigars.

Status:

A 1970s release. 1970-01-01

History:

Produced and gifted to government officials and selected VIPs. Some particularly special gifts will have standard custom bands printed with the recipient's name.

Brown

Cohiba VIP Gifts Cuban cigar.
