Official Weight:

GRM11.66g

Construction:

Handmade.

Bands:

Serie D No.4 band.

First bands had 3 periods; later bands 1 period (after No.). This was a suppliers error.

Premium tube.

Packaging:

Display box of 15 aluminium tubed cigars in 5 cardboard packs of 3 (released 2009).
