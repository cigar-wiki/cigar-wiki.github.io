History:

Humidor of 50 initially released in 2020 as a Limited Edition. A revised regular production version released in 2022. A limited-edition design packaging of 5 was also available in 2020.

Numbered humidors of 88 celebrating the Chinese Zodiac Year of the Ox, Year of the Rabbit, and Year of the Dragon were released in 2022, 2023, and 2024 respectively.
