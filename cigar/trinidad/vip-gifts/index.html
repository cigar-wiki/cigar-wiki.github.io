Gran Panetela - a special production size Narrow Gran Panetela band Standard Trinidad band A

Dimensions:

Ring gaugerg38 × LengthMMT192 mm(7½″)

Handmade.

Varnished boîte nature box of 25 cigars.

Cajón of 100 cigars.

A 1969 release. 1969-01-01Discontinued 1997. Extremely rare.
