Trinidad Fundadores Vintage 10

Vintage Series

Laguito Especial - an existing regular production size.

Cigar Name:

Fundadores

Factory Name:

Laguito Especial

Dimensions:

Ring gaugerg40 × LengthMMT192 mm(7½″)

Official Weight:

WeightGRM10.56g

Construction:

Handmade, then aged for 10-14 years.

Bands:

Standard band B, with a Vintage 10 band.

Packaging:

Numbered humidor of 55 cigars (1,000 produced).

Status:

A 2024 release. 2024-01-01Released mid-2024.

History:

These cigars were first announced to distributors for the Habanos Añejados programme in 2021, but later repurposed for the first release of the Vintage programme.

The humidors for these cigars are manufactured by S. T. Dupont. The first 55 humidors have an exclusive design from French fashion designer Charaf Tajer, to celebrate the 55th anniversary of the Trinidad brand.
