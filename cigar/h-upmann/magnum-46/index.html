H. Upmann Magnum 46

Current Regular Production Cigar

Magnum 46 Second Band

Cigar Name:

Magnum 46

Factory Name:

Coronas Gordas

Dimensions:

Ring gaugerg46 × LengthMMT143 mm(5⅝″)

Official Weight:

GRM11.41g

Construction:

Handmade.

Bands:

Before c2005 - no bands. Between c2005 & c2008 - standard band A, B, C. Since c2008 - standard band C, with a special Magnum 46 second band.

Tubes:

Tube introduced c2009.

Packaging:

Display box of 15 aluminium tubed cigars in 5 cardboard packs of 3 (released c2009).

Slide lid box of 25 cigars.

Slide lid box of 50 cigars (discontinued 2012).

Cardboard pack of 3 cigars (discontinued c2009).

Status:

A pre-1960 release. 1960-01-01Current.

History:

In 2010 a ceramic jar of 30 cigars were produced locally for Western Europe.

Although officially discontinued in 2012, 50 count boxes have been reported with dates as recently as May 2014.
