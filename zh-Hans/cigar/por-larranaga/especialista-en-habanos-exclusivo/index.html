Por Larrañaga Picadores No.1 Especialista en Habanos y La Casa del Habano Exclusivo

Habanos Specialist and La Casa del Habano Exclusive

Picadores No.1

Factory Name:

Hermosos No.4

Special Por Larrañaga band.

Packaging:

Dress box of 25 cigars (released 2014).

Status:

A 2014 release. 2014-01-01

History:

From launch, this cigar was named the Picadores on the boxes, and was also referred to as such in launch materials and press releases. The Habanos S.A. official catalogue, however, always called it the Picadores No.1, as did their barcode validation system. As of the 2023, the packaging has been redesigned, with the name on the boxes now appearing as Picadores No.1.

The special band was inspired by a 1970s design.
