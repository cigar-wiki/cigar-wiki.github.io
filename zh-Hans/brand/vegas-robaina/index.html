Brand Notes​

Status

Vegas Robaina is a current post-revolution brand, established in 1997. It is named for famous Cuban tobacco farmer Alejandro Robaina.

It is classified by Habanos SA in their 'Other' category of their brand portfolio. It receives occasional Regional Edition releases.

Generally

The range comprises medium to full strength cigars using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.

A 2008 release. 2008-01-01Not available until late 2009.

History:

This cigar was planned to be released under the La Gloria Cubana brand. Because of trade mark problems, the release was changed to Vegas Robaina. The already produced La Gloria Cubana boxes were re-branded by adding a Vegas Robaina metal name plate to the boxes.  There are pre-release cigars bearing the La Gloria Cubana band in circulation.
