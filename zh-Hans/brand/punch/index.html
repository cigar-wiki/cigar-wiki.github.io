Brand Notes​

Status

Punch is a current pre-revolution brand, established circa 1840.

It is classified by Habanos SA in their 'Value' category of Portfolio brands. Under the pre-2019 system it was considered a Multi-Local brand. It receives frequent Regional Edition and occasional other new release cigars. At one time Punch was a major brand, however its range was significantly reduced after the blue mold crisis of 1980 and again with the rationalisation of sizes in 2002.

Generally

The range comprises medium strength cigars, using tobacco from the premium Vuelta Abajo region.
