Brand Notes​

Status

Don Cándido is a Discontinued pre-revolution brand. It was created in 1935 by the owner of the El Rey del Mundo factory as a top-tier brand.

From the 1950s onward, it was exclusively distributed by Alfred Dunhill of London. It was discontinued in 1982 when the Cuban Dunhill brand was introduced.
